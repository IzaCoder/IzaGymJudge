Understanding gymnastics can be hard, but we make it easy.

We sell infographics and documents to help you understand women’s artistic gymnastics (the one that Simone Biles competes), and improve scores. Our products break down many aspects of gymnastics (like scoring) piece-by-piece, with illustrations to help your understanding.

Products

We design our products to help you understand gymnastics and improve scores.

Target 10 breaks down deductions for WDP (formerly JO) levels 1 to 5. Target 10s have illustrations of how each skill is supposed to look, along with things the gymnast can do to raise their score. Each deduction is color-coded by amount, and is the gymnast can achieve to improve their score (e.g., “straight legs” instead of “bent legs”).

Routine Makers help you create Xcel routines for each event that meet all Special Requirements (SRs).
